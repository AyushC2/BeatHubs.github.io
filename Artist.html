Karan Aujla
Karan Aujla, born as Jaskaran Singh Aujla on January 18, 1997, is an Indian singer, rapper, and songwriter primarily associated with Punjabi music. He hails from Ghurala village in Ludhiana, Punjab, India.He released his debut song “Property of Punjab” as a lead artist in 20161. He gained mainstream popularity in 2018 with “Don’t Worry”, his first song to enter the UK Asian chart

KR$NA
